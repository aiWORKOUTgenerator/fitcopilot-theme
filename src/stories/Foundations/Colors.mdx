## Usage Guidelines

- Use brand colors sparingly for maximum impact
- Ensure text has sufficient contrast with background colors (WCAG AA compliance minimum)
- Reserve status colors for their semantic meanings
- For gradients, combine primary and secondary colors in a left-to-right direction
